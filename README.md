Problem:
Briefly explain the real-world problem you are solving.:Real-World Problem Statement (Brief)
In many local areas, customers face difficulty in finding reliable and skilled service providers such as plumbers, electricians, tutors, or delivery agents. Most services are found through word-of-mouth, which is time-consuming and often unreliable.
At the same time, many local workers struggle to get enough work because they lack visibility and access to customers. There is no centralized platform that efficiently connects service providers with people who need their services.
This creates a gap between demand and supply, leading to inconvenience for customers and limited earning opportunities for service providers.
Solution:
Describe your approach and how your project solves it:Approach:
The project will be developed as a full-stack web platform that connects local service providers (plumbers, tutors, electricians, delivery agents, etc.) with customers in their area. The approach focuses on ease of access, trust, and real-time connectivity.
User-Friendly Interface
Customers can easily search for services based on location and category.
Service providers can create profiles, list their services, pricing, and availability.
Location-Based Matching
The platform uses geolocation to show nearby service providers, ensuring quick and relevant results.
Reduces time and effort for both customers and workers.
Secure Booking System
Customers can book services directly through the platform.
Includes scheduling, instant booking, and service tracking.
Ratings & Reviews System
Customers can rate and review service providers.
Helps build trust and ensures quality service.
Real-Time Communication
In-app chat or call feature for direct communication between customers and providers.
Helps clarify requirements before booking.
Secure Payment Integration
Multiple payment options (UPI, cards, wallets).
Ensures safe and transparent transactions.
Admin Dashboard
Admin can manage users, services, complaints, and monitor platform activity.
Helps maintain quality and resolve disputes.
